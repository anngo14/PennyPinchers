Budgeting Made Easy

There are a lot of things in life that are complicated and time comsuming, budgeting your money should not be one of them. Answer a few questions and have all the hard work done for you. Made with young adults in mind, Penny Pinchers is built to take all of the guess work out of managing your budget and automate the process for you.

Visualize Your Budget

Ditch the piles of static numbers on an excel spread sheet and see numbers come to life with charts and graphs. Visual cues make it easier for us to understand data at a glance so why not apply it to your budget. See your progress and track your financial goals to keep yourself accountable.

Watch Your Progress

Track your goals and your expenses with helpful visual cues. Watch your savings grow with your custom-made budget every month. An archive page is available that holds budget, expense, and goal information from previous months to review.
